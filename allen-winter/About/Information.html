Who is Allen Winter?

I'm a Japanese-American who is born, raised and resides in Long Island, NY. I discovered a love and courage for acting after watching Broadway shows and joining a school play in the 5th Grade. Once the acting bug hit, throughout my entire school life and in college I continued to learn more about acting and singing.

In the Summer of 2015 after I had just graduated, I decided to focus more on Voice Acting and found the right instructors to help me along the way. Since making that choice have been a working voice actor appearing in projects ranging from video games, visual novels, audio dramas, animations and anime.

Head over to the resume page for a full list of my projects. I also still perform on the stage, but more casually.

Outside of acting, I'm currently in the process of taking a degree in Computer Information Systems. I enjoy most nerdy things you can think of, animation/anime, dogs, watching baseball and hockey and traveling.
